BATTLE OF THE BANDS (INDIAN)


Rules

  • Time: 12+3 minutes (including setup and clearance time).
  • In case of failure to adhere to the allotted time limit, output volumes will be cut.
  • Minimum of 4 and Maximum of 8 members per team.
  • A basic 4 or 5 piece band with at least one Indian melodic or percussive Instrument is required to be eligible to participate in the competition. (Eg. Drummer, Bassist, Rhythm Guitarist, Lead Guitarist, Flutist and a Vocalist or Drummer Bassist, Guitarist, Keyboardist, Mridangam player and a Vocalist.)
  • Bands MUST contain at least ONE Indian Instrumentalist and at least one vocalist and not more than two vocalists. .
  • Lineup of the band does not matter if just the above rule is adhered to
  • A standard 5-piece Drum kit will be provided. (Departments have to arrange for Intra deanery competitions)
  • Growling is NOT allowed.
  • Individual Prizes for Best Original Composition, Best Vocalist, Best Keyboardist, Best Guitarist, Best Bassist, Best Drummer, and Best Indian (Melodic Instrumentalist) and Best Indian (Rhythm Instrumentalist). (Only for Finals)
  • Failure to adhere to the given rules of this event will result in disqualification.
  • First 2 winners of the prelims to be selected for the finals on 11 to 13 April 2023

Event Details

  • Student Coordinators: Vidya Shree B V & Lisa Shibu George

  • Date: Starting 20th March


For more information, contact the Faculty Coordinator or Student Coordinator.
Blossoms is the intra-deanery cultural fest at Christ University with the motto ‘Be the best’. This is a unique venture where all 7000 students of Christ University, Bangalore participate in a cultural event. The events are divided into four categories – Art, Theatre, Stage & Literary. Students compete in 23 events across these categories. The winners lay their hands on the Inter-Deanery Christ University trophy for Artistic Excellence and Service.
